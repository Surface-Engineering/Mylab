Openings

We are recruiting motivated students and researchers to work on surface engineering, tribology, and printed electronics.

Open Positions

We are hiring — effective immediately

A M.S., Ph.D., or integrated M.S.–Ph.D. student, or a full-time researcher, is sought for open research positions. A separate post-doctoral position is also available.

Graduate Students & Researchers

M.S., Ph.D., or integrated M.S.–Ph.D. students, and full-time researchers, to lead experimental projects in the lab.

Post-Doctoral Researcher

A postdoc with expertise in conductive coatings, EMI shielding, EHD jet printing, electrospray, or device characterization.

Research Directions

Project areas

MXene EMI Shielding Coatings

MXene-based electromagnetic-interference shielding coatings fabricated via electrospray deposition.

MXene Microsupercapacitors

MXene-based micro-supercapacitors fabricated by electrohydrodynamic jet printing.

High-Resolution Memristors

High-resolution memristors fabricated by electrohydrodynamic jet printing.

Graduate students — qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in materials science, chemical, mechanical, or electrical engineering, or a related field.
  • Preferred experience with solution-processed nanomaterials, thin-film processes, printed electronics, microfabrication, and material/electrical characterization.

Postdoc — qualifications

  • Ph.D. in materials science, chemical, mechanical, or electrical engineering, or a related discipline.
  • Expertise in conductive coatings, EMI shielding, EHD jet printing, electrospray, micro/nanofabrication, or device characterization.

Responsibilities

Mentorship of undergraduate students, presentations at international conferences, and writing publications for peer-reviewed scientific journals.
